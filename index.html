Jobless

At the ripe age of 15, I developed a type of, if I may call it, performance anxiety, which has only worsened as the years have progressed. Despite such predicament, looming societal expectations have been weighing on my shoulders since the day I entered this world, resulting in me being where I am now: attending a university as a full-time student.

As for job experiences, I have none. Though I have balanced academics, extracurriculars, and community service during my youth, I have recently been investing my time outside of school to keep myself alive and functioning to brace the next day. After experiencing burnout after burnout, I realized how critical it is to ensure I stop jeopardizing my health for the remainder of my life.

I am currently not in the search for a job, nor am I interested in making a resume on a site like this that I find unfamiliar. The sole purpose of this would be to finish a lab that is required of me. In truth, I find it extremely uncomfortable to be forced into writing my personal information on a platform like this.
